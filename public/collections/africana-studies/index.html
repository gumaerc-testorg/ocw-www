Africana Studies

In collaboration with a network of Historically Black Colleges and Universities (HBCUs), MIT OpenCourseWare has curated this collection of OCW courses related to Africana, African American, and Black Studies topics.  

Learn more about OCW’s collaboration with HBCUs and MERLOT in this blog post, and see this MIT OpenCourseWare Africana Studies course collection also on the MERLOT website. This work is part of the HBCU Affordable Learning Solutions Cultural Collections, and is one of the initiatives of the HBCU Affordable Learning Solutions (AL$) Community Portal.
